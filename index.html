We are a global company helping entrepreneurs build companies. In partnership with founders, we develop minimal viable products (MVPs) to validate business opportunities, and build teams to scale the business independently. Product-based planning is intended to ensure that all of the necessary products are identified and captured, and begins by identifying a product breakdown structure which is then repeatedly refined until all of the requisite products are identified. The PBS is thus a hierarchical family tree of all the products and sub-products that comprise the final end-product.
